Venue & Travel Information

Gedung Indonesia Menggugat
Jalan Perintis Kemerdekaan No 5, Bandung

Well, where else it's would be? It is Bandung Open, anyway. ;)

How to Reach the Venue by Using Public Transport

  • From Bandung Train Station: You can just walk about 2 km to the east and found Jalan Perintis Kemerdekaan. But if you insists on public transport, go to South gate of the station and take St-Hall-Dago angkot, then get off on Jalan Perintis Kemerdekaan.
  • From Kiara Condong Train Station: take a local train (about every 30 minutes or so) to Bandung Train Station then follow the direction above.
  • From Husein Sastranegara Airport: There is no public transportation but taxi, the price should be around 20-25k Rupiah
  • From Cicaheum Bus Station: Take Cicaheum-Ciroyom angkot, get off on Simpang Dago junction (there is a McDonald’s in the junction) then take Dago-St-Hall angkot, get off at Bank Indonesia Braga, and walk few steps to the west.
  • From Leuwi Panjang Bus Station: Take Leuwi Panjang – Dipati Ukur DAMRI city bus, you can get off in front of the venue.
  • You can try to find the route of the angkot from other locations by using the KIRI.TRAVEL website
