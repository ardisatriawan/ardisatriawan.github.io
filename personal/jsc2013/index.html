Jakarta Speedcubing Competition 2013

Information | Venue | Events | Schedule | Registration

Jakarta Speedcubing Competition 2013 is a speedcubing competition organized by Jakarta Rubik's Cube Club. The person in charge for this competition is Arief Widodo and the WCA delegate is Ardianto Satriawan. It will take place on 8 September 2013 in Jakarta, Indonesia. The registration fee is IDR 100,000. You can pay by bank transfer to Haryo Trisulo (BCA 5255155391) then please confirm by text message to him, +628889032134.

Venue

The venue of this competition is:
WTC Mangga Dua
Jalan Mangga Dua Raya No. 8 Jakarta
Indonesia

You can view venue map on google.

Events

Events will be held in this competition are:
  • 2x2
  • 3x3
  • 3x3 One Handed
  • 3x3 Blindfolded
  • 3x3 With Feet
  • Pyraminx
